Agra Package
Day-1  (Delhi > Agra > Delhi)
This morning embark on a drive to Agra, land of the Taj Mahal.

This afternoon, witness the surreal views of the Taj Mahal, sparkling in the glittering rays of the Indian sun. Step inside this eternal symbol of love and appreciate the intricate designs and symmetries that rhyme as a poem written in marble.

Also visit the splendid Agra fort, built in red sandstone, encapsulating magnificent palaces, halls of public and private audience and beautiful gardens.

Late afternoon embark on your return drive to Delhi.
